Experience

Portfolio Managers and Analysts work together to manage investments in our $600,000 Alpha Fund. Our Beta Fund has about $140,000 in private assets under management that our quant traders proceed to trade in options.

Equity Research & Stock Pitches

Analysts will take part in stock pitches of which the winners will get prizes and a portion of the assets in the Alpha Fund will be invested into the winning pitches' companies.

Guest Speakers

Guest speakers provide our analysts with advice from experts in their respective fields. Our speaker list includes senior corporate officials from Disney and Amazon.

Networking

Connect with members, mentors, and alumni to build meaningful relationships and explore career opportunities in finance. The club fosters a collaborative environment encouraging valuable connections for career development.

Who Are We

Bruin Stock Group is a student-created undergraduate investment organization. Created in 2020, our founders are extremely passionate about teaching others the intricacies of how to how, what, and where to invest. We advise the allocation of more than $600,000 in private assets under management, within our Alpha Fund and Beta Fund, which we like to use to teach our members about real-world investing.
